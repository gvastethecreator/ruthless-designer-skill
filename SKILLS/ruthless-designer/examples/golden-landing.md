# Golden Landing Direction

Use as a compact reference for brand or hybrid work. The page persuades through artifact and proof, not section costume.

## Brief

Landing page for a local architectural lighting studio selling custom fixtures to boutique hotels.

## Direction

- Killed default: centered luxury hero with beige background and three feature cards.
- Killed second reflex: monochrome editorial page with oversized serif drama.
- Selected direction: artifact stage built from fixture geometry and warm/cool material contrast.
- Signature move: light falloff proof. Each section pairs a fixture crop with the room effect it creates.
- Composition: artifact-first hero plus proof wall.
- Dials: variance 7, motion 4, density 3.

## System

- Hero: real fixture image or generated bitmap, product name, concise offer, consultation CTA, hint of room proof below.
- Proof: before/after room lighting, material swatches, fabrication lead time, maintenance objection, hospitality case quote.
- Palette: derived from brushed metal, smoked glass, warm bulb core, and cool wall shadow.
- Type: restrained sans for claims, compact serif or display family only for project names.
- States: mobile hero crop, missing image fallback, contact form errors, long hotel/project names.

## Proof

- First viewport shows actual fixture/product category, not abstract luxury.
- CTA remains visible on small laptop and mobile.
- Image edges are inspectable with neutral outlines or deliberate full bleed.
- No fake logos, fake metrics, or placeholder customer names.
